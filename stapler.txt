The following can be taken as possible unit test cases for a stapler:

-if there are pins in the stapler or is it empty.
-if the size of the pins available match the size required by the stapler.
-if the stapler is damaged in any way or not.
-if the pins are damaged in any way or are ready to be loaded.
-if the max no. of pins that can be inserted is reached or is there more space for the pins.
-max no. of pages that it can pin in one action.
